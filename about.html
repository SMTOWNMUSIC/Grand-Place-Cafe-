About Us

Grand Place Cafe is a cafe that opened in California and has since expanded it's business with two new locations on the East Coast. One location is in New York and the other location is in Pennsylvania.

Opening up two new Grand Place Cafe locations in New York and Pennsylvania turned out to be a smart business decision due to people from across the globe traveling in and out of New York and Pennsylvania day to day.

In addition, these two countries already have a big population count itself which means that conducting business in these areas will be an advantage in reaching success at a fast rate and also attracting customers from different cultures as well

This cafe has made it a priority to only serve food and beverage selections that are homemade and organic to customers as a way of standing out from other cafe shops all over the world.

This cafe strives to provide the best dining experience for every customer that comes in even if it's their first time here.

It's mission is to always serve fresh healthy and organic food selections to customers.

This will create an impact on how our world will transform and convince everyone across the globe to "Go Green"



Grand Place Cafe is a paradise for people who are big fans of all breakfast selections imagined. Breakfast selections at this cafe ranges from homemade pancakes down to homemade waffles which comes with a free cup of any size coffee

We plan on continuing to make our food and beverage selections organic and healthy for all customers that come to visit.
