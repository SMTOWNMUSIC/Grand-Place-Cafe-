Welcome to Grand Place Cafe!

Do you love ice cream? Or are you a fan of breakfast? If you are someone who likes both of those selections then you've come to the right cafe!

At this cafe we strive to go beyond out customers expectations in creating everything from scratch, homemade, and organic.
